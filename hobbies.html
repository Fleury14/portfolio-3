Resume
Free Enterprise

A randomizer for the SNES classic Final Fantasy IV, I've been a workshop helper for this community for over three years helping organize and run events as well as a technical outlet for displaying tournament and racer data. I thoroughly enjoy races other randomizers as well.

Hockey

For over 20 years I have participated in an in-house hockey league at Gateway Ice Center. I started playing ice hockey in 1997 and have enjoyed it since. Unfortunately, COVID brought a halt to that (as it did many things), but I am looking forward to returning to regular play.

Fighting Games

Around 2010 soon after the release of Street Fighter IV, I joined a local community of fighting game players. Since then, we have traveled to events and hosted events locally, and I've made some of my closest friends through this activity. Nowadays with the release of Street Fighter 6, a renewed fervor for improving in the subtle ways that a fighting game can test you has served me well.
